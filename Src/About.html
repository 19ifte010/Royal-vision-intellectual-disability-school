Our Vision

Empowerment of the differently abled is our vision. The first and foremost step to achieve this target is to make them holistic human beings through proper education and training which would make them self confident and self-reliant so as to lead a life of self – respect and self – dignity.

Our Mission

To render selfless service to society in general and the differently abled in particular through the mobilization of available resources from national and international philanthropists is our mission. To unearth the inherent talents of the hitherto ignored or neglected potential of the differently abled by motivating them to set a side inhibition so as to face the society with the god gifted abilities.

Our Objectives

  • To spread light and happiness in the life of the differently disabled.
  • To raise their quality of life through necessary education, skill training and employment.
  • To take care of their health problems through regular, periodical medical camps.
  • Creation and sustenance of awareness about health hazards including AIDS and cancer through regular and periodical interaction with theoretical and practical experts in the field.
  • To establish an audio library for the assistance of the visually impaired.
  • To make arrangements for a short but comfortable stay of the differently abled.
  • To form and maintain SHG's in order to empower the differently abled.
  • To make arrangements for micro credit facilities to encourage the differently abled to become self-employed/entrepreneurs.
  • To establish/run a home for the aged with visual impairements.
